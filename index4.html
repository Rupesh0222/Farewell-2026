Kal tak jo raaste saath chale,

Aaj un par yaadein reh jayengi.

Classroom ki hasi, playground ki baatein,

Bas tasveeron me simat jayengi.

Aaj hum 11th wale,

Dil se ek baat kehna chahte hain,

Seniors nahi, parivaar ho tum,

Ye ehsaas dilana chahte hain.

Is farewell ki shaam me,

Sirf alvida nahi, shukriya hoga,

Saath bitaye har pal ke liye,

Har seekh, har kahaani ke liye.

Toh aaiye seniors,

Is khaas shaam ka hissa baniye,

Jahan khushi bhi hogi,

Aur aankhon me nami bhi.
